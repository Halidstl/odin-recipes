Chicken Alfredo

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • Half a box of fettuccine pasta
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 cup of Alfredo Sauce
  • Love

Steps

  1. Boil the water in a medium sauce pan.
  2. add pasta to water.
  3. Cook the chicken.
  4. take out pasta and chicken and combine.
  5. add the sauce to the pasta and chicken.
  6. enjoy!
