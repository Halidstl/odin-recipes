Parmesan Risotto

Description

The creamiest risotto you have ever tasted. It will melt in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • Half a box of Arborio Rice
  • 1 quart of chicken stock
  • Block of parmesan
  • 2 shallots

Steps

  1. boil the chicken stock in medium sauce pan.
  2. add the arborio rice in a different pot and toast the grains.
  3. once toasted add the shallots to the arborio and cook until soft and translucent.
  4. start adding the chicken stock one ladel at a time until evaporated.
  5. keep adding the chicken stock until it is gone.
  6. add the parmesan to the risotto and stir it in.
  7. plate it up and enjoy!
