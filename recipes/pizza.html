Cheese Pizza

Description

This cheese pizza recipe is simple and easy to follow. It is a quick and easy meal to prepare and you probably have most of the ingredients at home.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • 1 1/3 cups of Water
  • 1 Packet of Yeast
  • 2 Tbsp of Garlic Powder
  • 3 Tbsp of Olive Oil
  • 1 can of Tomato Sauce
  • Lots of Love

Steps

  1. Put the yeast in a bowl and combine it with water. Let it sit for 15 minutes at room temperature.
  2. Add the flour to the bowl and combine.
  3. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes.
  4. add your olive oil and garlic powder on the shaped dough.
  5. add the sauce on top.
  6. bake and enjoy.
